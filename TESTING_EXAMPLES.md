```

- "I love it but also hate it"
- "It's great but disappointing"

Sarcasm (hard for AI):
- "Oh great, another problem"
- "Yeah, that's just perfect" (said negatively)
```

### 4. **Compare Before/After Reset**
- Have a conversation
- Note the context
- Click "Reset Conversation"
- Try same topics
- Responses will be different!

## ðŸ› Troubleshooting

### If responses seem weird:
1. **Click "Reset Conversation"**
   - Clears memory
   - Starts fresh

2. **Check if models are loaded**
   - Look for warning messages
   - Should see "Response generated by DialoGPT AI model"

3. **Try shorter messages**
   - Very long messages can confuse the model
   - Keep under 100 words for best results

### If sentiment seems wrong:
1. **Check confidence score**
   - Low confidence (<0.6) = model unsure
   - Ambiguous messages get lower scores

2. **Try clearer sentiment**
   - "I hate this" instead of "Not my favorite"
   - "I love it!" instead of "It's nice"
